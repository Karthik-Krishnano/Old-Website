About Karthik

Karthik Krishnan is a First Year Computer Science Engineering with Specialisation in Data Science Student at Vellore Institute of Technology ,Vellore, Tamil Nadu. His hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has contributed to his recent successes. He is a good team player, tech enthusiast and a highly motivated self learner. He is a beginner and has a long way to go.

Born on 22nd March 2002 at Payyannur, Kerala, he spent most of his time in his native.

Was introduced to the world of programmming at the age of 16 with his first "Hello World" in Python. Since then he had a busy time learning to code.

He has very few friends and they always had a great role in shaping his skills.His family is his greatest strength. The respect he showed towards his teachers always helped him to get the most out of them.

Karthik has good experience in and mostly works on Frontend Web Devolpment and Python Programming. He also has good knowledge in C, C++, R, Matlab and MySQL.

He loves working on projects and also has worked on many personally as well as in a team.

He wishes to be a good knowledged devoloper who could solve the problems of the world with his thought and skills.
